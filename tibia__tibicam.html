TibiCam

back

TibiCam, first published around 2004, is TibiaCam.tv's predecessor.
Unlike TibiaCam.tv it uses the .REC format and is slightly more difficult to run.
You CANNOT use it to record anymore, but you'll need it to watch old .RECs.

Playback:
-extract the .rar file
-put the .REC file(s) into your TibiCam folder
-start Tibia (respective version)
-start TibiCam
-click "activate", then "play" on the Tibicam window
-"log in" on Tibia leaving account data blank

Note:
.REC file names cannot be longer than ~20 characters or TibiCam will crash
