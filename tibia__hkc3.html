HKC3 - HotkeyChanger 3

back

HKC3 allows you to hotswap between up to 10 different hotkey sets just by clicking a desktop shortcut.

Features:
-10 hotkey sets
-optional: MC mode (you don't have to shut all Tibia clients down before switching hotkey sets)
-optional: Silent mode (DOS boxes close automatically after switching hotkey sets)
-optional: Boot mode (Tibia starts up automatically after switching hotkey sets)
-better backup/restore function
-improved backup/restore function for both hotkeys and mapfiles
-VIP Data (icon, comment, login notification) is shared between the different sets
-no risk of client-related data loss, since everything is saved in %APPDATA%
-if your config is wiped this is auto-detected and fixed

System Requirements:
-Windows XP, Vista or 7
-System language* English, German, Dutch, Swedish, Norwegian, Finnish, Spanish (ES/MX/CH) or Portuguese (PT/BR)
-20-100MB disk space on your system drive (C: usually) for the mapfiles backup
-Vista's UAC might be a bitch about it. Win7's won't.

*It depends on the name of your desktop folder, actually.
If your language is not on the list, just try it, might work.

Installation:
-extract the .rar file
-put the HKC3 folder into your Tibia folder (eg. C:\Program Files\Tibia\HKC3)
-run hkc3.bat once, press a key whenever prompted
-read the 'setup' and 'quickstart' chapters in the manual. DO IT.
-you can now run HKC3 from your desktop
