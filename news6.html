Small Problem with HotkeyChanger
Uploaded the wrong version here when I changed hosts, so if you downloaded it after I switched to najko.com, re-download and overwrite your files. But yeh, going to make a newer, better and easier version within the next week anyway.

Careful when watching olders .cams
This is not a HKC-related problem but affects everybody. When watching a 8.11 or older cam with the new 2.0 TibiaCam.TV lite for Tibia 8.2 your config/hotkey set just gets erased*. Can't do much against it, but here's my solution* to it. Took about 30 seconds to make so don't expect it to be secure against YOU not following what I'm going to write now:

Instructions:
-run backup.bat
-watch the old cam(s)
-run restore.bat

Another way would be opening a Tibia client of version 8.2 or above, then watching the old cam(s) on an 8.0/8.1/8.11 MC client, closing the cam, then closing your real client because config is saved whenever you close a client.
