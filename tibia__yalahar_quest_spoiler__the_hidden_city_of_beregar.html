The Hidden City of Beregar Quest Spoiler

back

Rewards: Gold ingot, crystal of focus, access to the dwarven mines, ability to buy rust remover
Requirements: pick, chocolate (or chocolate cake), 3 iron ore, 3 bast skirts OR sweaty cyc quest done

The way to Beregar
-Find NPC Maris at the docks in the Foreign Quarter.
-Travel to Mistrock ('hi', 'mistrock', 'yes').
-Speak to Maris on Mistrock, 'hi', 'mistrock'.
-He will tell you there might be something strange under Fenrock.
-Travel to Fenrock.
-Go inside the mountain, down to -2 with turtles and crocodiles.
-Find the cracks in the stone floor in the southeastern corner and use your pick on them.
-Find your way through a place OT'er than OTs.
-Go down once more until you see Dwarven NPCs (near goblins).
-Go up the other stairs and activate the ore wagon. Will save you the walk in the future.
-Speak to the NPCs on the main floor and accept their missions.


Mission: Going Down
-As Xorlosh at the elevator for a 'mission'.
-If you don't have gear wheels (lootable from worker/war golems) go to the sweaty cyclops in Ab.
-Exchange three pieces of iron ore for gear wheels ('hi','gear wheel').
-Deliver them to Xorlosh by saying 'mission' again.


Mission: Justice for All
-Speak to Nokmir at the entrance to the northern mine.
-Say 'hi', 'mission', 'ring', 'grombur', 'rerun', 'rehal'.
-Go up the 3x stairs and Speak to emperor Rehal, 'hail emperor', 'nokmir', 'ring', 'rerun', 'promotion','grombur'.
-Speak to Grombur at the entrance to the Southern mine, 'nokmir','ring','rerun','promotion'.
-Enter the eastern mine (past the elevator), you need to have completed the 'Going Down' before.
-Find the beer cask in the northwestern corner of the mine and rightclick the crystals right next to it.
-You will find the ring, but unless it was a bug you won't see green text, so check your inventory and bp.
-Bring the ring to the emperor, 'hail emperor', 'ring'...
-Speak to Nokmir again, 'mission','acquitted'.


Mission: Sweet as Chocolate Cake
-Ask Frafnar at the entrance to the western mine for a 'mission'.
-Bake a chocolate cake if you don't have one. (use flour on milk, chocolate on dough, chocolate dough on oven).
-Go up the 3x stairs in the main room, west halfway to the emperor and find the bar.
-Speak to Bolfona, 'hi', 'chocolate cake', 'yes', 'Frafnar'.
-Report back to Frafnar.


Mission: The Good Guard
-Ask Grombur at the entrance to the southern mine for a 'mission'.
-Go to Boozer in Venore and ask for 'dwarven brown ale'.
-If you haven't done the Tibia Tales quest 'The perfect booze', you have to do it first ('mission').
-Deliver the cask of ale to Grombur by saying 'mission'.


Once done with all of these:
You can now speak to the dwarf NPC to the east, halfway to the emperor and buy rust remover to use on rusty armor/legs.
With each mission you finish you also get access to the respective mine. Nothing much in there but dwarves, really.
There is a 'Gold Ingot' in the eastern mine (past the elevator) and a 'Crystal of Focus' in the western mine.
Both of them are found in skeletons or crates/boxes. I haven't found out what they are good for, maybe another quest.
Oh, and there's 3 small topazs in a dead human in the northern mine.
