TibiaCam.tv

back

Version 3.1, compatible with Tibia 8.54 and under.
TibiaCam.tv uses two separate programs for recording and playback.
The official website is at TibiaCam.tv.

You can only use the recorder if you have a valid key, don't bother downloading if you don't.
You can, however, use somebody else's secondary key.

If you don't have a legit key, you may want to take a look at TibiaLive -- REMOVED instead.
It offers the same features as TibiaCam.tv Pro and records .CAM movies. For free.
