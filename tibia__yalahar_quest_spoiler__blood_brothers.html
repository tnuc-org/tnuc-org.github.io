Blood Brothers Quest Spoiler

back

Rewards: Access to Vengoth and the castle, Yalaharian Addon
Requirements: Level 40, blessed wooden stake, firebug, garlic, holy water, flour, garlic necklace?, three more people on the same mission for mission 5, one female char for mission 9

Mission 1: Gaining Trust
-Go to Julius in the Trade Quarter and speak about 'vampires' and a 'mission'.
-He will want you to prove yourself not to be a vampire.
-You automatically gain his trust if you have the rank 'Vampire hunter' or higher in the Inquisition quest -If not, say 'hi' whilst wearing a garlic necklace.
-Bake a garlic bread of holy water (Inq)+flour+garlic.
-Speak to Julius again, eat the bread and tell him 'Aaah'.


Mission 2: Bad Eggs
-Ask Julius for a 'mission'.
-Buy garlic in the Ab'dendriel fruit shop (northeast) or Ank.
-Bake garlic cookies (use flour on holy water, dough on garlic, garlic dough on baking tray, tray on oven).
-Speak to 2 NPCs in the Centre, 1 in the Trade Quarter, 1 in the Magician Quarter (floor+1) and 1 in the Foreign Quarter (floor+1) saying 'cookie','yes'.

-Report their names to Julius after saying 'mission', 'yes','name1','name2'.
-Names are Maris, Serafin, Lisander, Ortheus and Armenius.


Mission 3: His True Face
-Ask Julias for another 'mission'.
-He will give you the fake spell ALORI MORT.
-Say that to the Armenius in the trade quarter you gave the cookie to before. TWICE.
-He will tp you into the sewers.
-Run back to Julius and report in.


Mission 4: The Dark Lands
-Ask Julius for another 'mission'.
-Go to the docks straight south of Julius and tell the NPC there to bring you to Vengoth.
-Walk around and use the map Julius gave you near at least 5 of 8 possible important spots (see map).
-To get the the castle, go upstairs southeast, follow the path and use the whirlwind things as teleports.

-Report back to Julius.


Mission 5: Into the Castle
-Ask Julius for another 'mission'.
-Ask the temple npc (west of the boat) about the 'blood crystal'.
-Enter the cults area in the Magician Quarter and go down to the second last floor.
-Get the Blood Crystal from behind the sealed door.
-Report back to Julius.
-Go back to Vengoth and find the Soul/Ghost NPC in the ruins (surface).
-Say 'hi', 'blood crystal'.
-Report back to Julius.
-Now you need three others who are as far as you. Have each stand on one of the four tiles (black crosses).
-Once all are in position you will get teleported into the castle and lose your Blood Crystal.
-Leave and report back to Julius.


Mission 6: A Black History
-Ask Julius for another 'mission'.
-Go back to Vengoth and into the castle.
-In the middle of the hall there is a 2 sqm carpet.
-'Use' both of its tiles once to open and reveal a trapdoor and go down.
-Only one of the sealed doors will open for you, pass it.
-Go upstairs and get Marziel's diary from a bookshelf.
-Report back to Julius.


Mission 7: Boreth
-Take your firebug and ask Julius for another 'mission'.
-Go down the trapdoor in the castle again and threw the newly openable door.
-Proceed to the top until you reach a small room with a throne.
-Use the firebug on the bushes in front of it until you get teleported.
-Defeat Boreth and use the blessed wooden stake on his remains.
-Bring Boreth' dust to Julius.


Mission 8: Lersatio
-Ask Julius for another 'mission' and get back down the trapdoor.
-You are now able to open yet another door.
-Make your way to the top again until you reach the throne room.
-Break (use) the mirrors next to the throne to summon Lersatio.
-Kill him, use the stake and bring his dust to Julius.


Mission 9: Marziel
-'mission' and down the trapdoor once again. You know you love repetition.
-Go through the last door and up to the top.
-Have a female char step up to the throne, then pour blood on that sqm and wait ~15sec.
-Kill, use stake, bring dust to Julius.


Mission 10: Arthei
-Get you 'mission' to kill the last of the brothers.
-Past the fourth door and right past the next sealed door instead of going up.
-Make your way to the top.
-Speak to the ghostly female NPC below the throne room.
-'Kala', 'diary', 'yes', 'vampire', EITHER 'free soul' OR 'kill', 'awaken'.
-She will give you 'a ghost's tear'.
-Use it on the last throne twice and wait ~15 seconds.
-Defeat Arthei and bring his dust to Julius.
-Once done, Julius will give you a 'vampiric crest', which after saying 'addon' to Palimuth you can exchange for your addon.

Note:
the type of addon is determined by the faction you choose in "In Service of Yalahar - Mission 9".
