Stopped working with the latest YouTube re-design, just use JDownloader.
JDownloader is a popular download manger specialised in hosting sites like rapidshare.
Paste a YouTube link into JDownloader and it will download and convert the video automatically.

Ever wanted to download YouTube videos without installing extra software or registering on dubious websites?
It's as simple as creating a JavaScript bookmark and clicking it whilst on a YouTube video page.
Simply drag the button below onto your bookmarks bar:

Download this Video


Your browser doesn't have a bookmarks bar?
Create a new blank bookmark and copy this into the URL field:

Now, when on a YouTube page, click your bookmark to start downloading.
If the player has fully loaded the video already, download will be instantaneous.

Output format is .FLV, flash video, played by most of the more popular media players like VLC.
If you want the sound/music only, look for a 'FLV to MP3 Converter'.
