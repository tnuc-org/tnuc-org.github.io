Ventrilo and Vista

back

Running WinXP programmes on Vista's default settings spawns millions of unnecessary errors.
You can't speak on vent?
You can't hear others?
You can't connect properly?

Chances are it's Vista's fault. Fuck you, Vista.

Rightclick your Ventrilo shortcut and go to 'Properties'.
In the 'Compatibility' tab check both of the following:
-Run this program as an Administrator
-Run this program in compatibility mode for 'Windows XP Service Pack 2'

Et voilà. 90% of possible vent problems are solved.
