Pythius the Rotten Quest Spoiler

back

Requirements: a golden mug, pick
Rewards: Firewalker boots (lv 130+, make you lose 2hp only when stepping on a firefield)

Note: You must have access to deeper Fenrock before starting.
Take a look at the 'Hidden City of Beregar' spoiler for more information on that.

-Find Pythius the Rotten (up and east from Beregar, until you find dragons and hatchlings).
-Say 'hi', 'offer', 'yes', 'golden mug' and he will allow you to enter the teleport once.
-Walk into the teleport, go downstairs and head west.
-You'll see a large stone at the southern end of the western wall of the room with water dripping from the wall.
-Use your pick on the stone to make another teleport appear.
-Once you walk in (only one person at a time it seems), you'll face Pythius live.
-He summons two undead gladiators and uses some sort of small energy gfb on himself.
-From what I saw the max damage output per turn was around 800-1000. He's WAY weaker than an undead dragon.
-Once he's dies you are teleported into the reward room (the western side of his treasure).
-Take the firewalker boots from the chest and leave.
