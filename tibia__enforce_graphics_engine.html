Force Tibia to use a certain Graphics Engine

back

I'm not too sure why, but a lot of you are having trouble with either DirectX or OpenGL and Tibia always starting in a certain graphics mode while you explicitely chose a different one.

But don't worry, there's a simple fix to that one as well.

-Rightclick your desktop/start menu shortcut to Tibia and go to 'Properties'.
-Go to the second tab ('Shortcut').
-Add " engine X", without the quotation marks, at the end of the path, where X is one of the following:

  • 0 = DirectX 5
  • 1 = OpenGL
  • 2 = DirectX 6

This will force Tibia to start up with the mode in the shortcut path.
