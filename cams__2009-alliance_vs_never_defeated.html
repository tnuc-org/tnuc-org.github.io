After Aristaeus is disbanded its members join back to Enclave (Bysen), Invisus (Arwyn) and Loathe (Fizel).
On Dec 11th 2008 they start randoming again. An ultimatum is set, forcing Maelstrom to pay for a fake reason.
After Maelstrom announce that they will not pay, Bysen and Joshiko (old Aeldene) found the guild Brandbilen.
Many members of Enclave, Invisus and Loathe join Brandbilen; the remains of the guilds stay "neutral".
On December 13th Brandbilen declare war on Maelstrom by killing Korragorrax. Maelstrom ally with DL again.
