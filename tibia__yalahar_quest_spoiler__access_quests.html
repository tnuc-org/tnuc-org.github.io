Access Quests

back

Access to Yalahar:
-Do the first (pickaxe) mission of the Explorer Society Quest if you haven't done so yet.
-Speak to Wyrdin in Edron (middle spell tower) and ask about 'yalahar' and 'mission'.
-Go to Captain Max in LB (Calassa Captain) and say 'hi' 'yalahar' 'yes'.
You can now use that route whenever you want.

Once in Yalahar, find Timothy near the depot, ask about 'research notes' (optional).
Go back to the Captain in the second boat at the docks and say 'passage'.
He will give you the mission to show him 'special' things from different towns.
Doing so will allow you to travel from/to Yalahar directly from other cities.

You have to bring at least 5 out of 7 possible items.
Note: all of these are found on TRADE menus, not by talking.

Thais:
-Go to Gamon at the northeast end of town and buy a 'piece of royal satin'.

Carlin:
-Go to the flower shop right south of the temple and buy a 'bowl of evergreen flowers'.

Darashia:
-Go to the northeastern food NPC at the market place and buy a 'sample of sandwasp honey'.

Ankrahmun:
-Buy a jug of embalming fluid at the mana shop.

Port Hope
-There is nothing to buy, just fix an elephant tusk.

Venore
-Buy a 'spice sample' at the fruit shop east of the boat.

Ab'dendriel
-Buy bug milk at the fruit shop all northeast.

Once you have five of these return to the captain, say 'passage'.
Then name all the cities the items of which you gathered followed by 'yes'.
After the fifth item he will allow you to travel.

The research notes Timothy gave you for Wyrdin are not necessary for the access quests,
but upon handing them in Wyrdin will give you 500gp.
This is also needed to mark the "In Service of Yalahar" quest as fully completed.
