Wartool Lite.

Mar 28 2010

Okay, it's done.
Not as fast as planned but work's been keeping me busy all week.

Wartool lite is a manageable online list, much like Guildcheck but with indidual characters instead of entire guilds. Characters are divided into two customisable categories (i.e. Enemy Main and Second Chars, Allies and Enemies or Friends and Hunted People). Use it for war, as a guild blackbook or just to extend your in-game VIP list.

Unlike Guildcheck, Wartool Lite caches character level and vocation of online characters and recalls them when the character is offline. Quite a step up from the other scripts on the site, but a fucking joke compared to our Wartool 2.0, which would keep chars up to date automatically, logs deaths, logins and online times and have a phone list, user groups, character status groups, access quests, and whatnot.

Sucks I won't ever have the time/motivation to fix and share it, but there wouldn't be much of a gain either seeing I quit long ago, I'd have to update servers to host it myself, and I don't even have ads on the site.

But yeh, why don't you just go take a look?

-F
