How to MC Ventrilo

back

By default Ventrilo won't allow you to open multiple instances, but there's a fast and easy workaround.

-Rightclick your Vent shortcut (Desktop or Start menu usually)
-Click 'Properties'


-Go to the 'Shortcut' tab
-Add a blankspace, a dash and an m at the back of the 'Target' path ( -m)


-Apply changes, close, DONE.
